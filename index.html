Cheese and Vegi Roti Bowls




The “Roti Bowls with Cheese and Vegetables” is a great snack for when you want a simple snack but want to make it yourself to feel proud of yourself. Guests will also love it, as it is a twist on a traditional Indian meal, roti with curry. This recipe saves you the hassle of making a curry and requires only a bowl, a plate, and a pan, which reduces the amount of time you spend washing dishes. I really hope you enjoy the recipe, as it is my own creation that I love, and hope you will, too!




Ingredients:

    Roti/Chapati flour mix (3 cups)

    Hot water ( 1 cup)

    Shredded Cheese (desired amount)

    Tomato (optional)

    Spinach (optional)
